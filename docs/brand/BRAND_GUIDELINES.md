# DGN-DJ Studio Brand Guidelines

## Naming

- **Official Name**: DGN-DJ Studio
- **CLI Prefix**: `studio` or `dgn-dj`
- **Avoid**: "Automation", "RoboDJ", "Legacy Core" (unless technical necessity)

## Color Palette (Proposed)

- **Primary**: Deep Studio Purple (#4B0082)
- **Secondary**: Neon Cyan (#00FFFF) for "On Air" states.
- **Background**: Sleek Dark Mode (#121212).

## Typography

- **Headings**: Orbitron (Modern, futuristic)
- **Body**: Inter / Roboto

## Voice & Tone

- **Tone**: Professional, broadcast-ready, yet high-tech and intelligent.
- **Language**: Use radio terms (Dayparts, Clockwheels, Sweepers, Bumper) combined with AI terms (Embeddings, Autonomy Levels, Decision Logs).
