# DGN-DJ Studio: Unified Vision

## The Mission

To provide a seamless, AI-powered radio broadcasting experience that combines legacy automation reliability with modern autonomy.

## Brand Shift: Automation -> Studio

- **From**: "DGN-DJ Automation" (Focus: Just playing files)
- **To**: "DGN-DJ Studio" (Focus: Creative orchestration, AI hosting, and intelligent scheduling)

## Core Focus Areas

1. **Autonomy**: High-level station control with human-in-the-loop checkpoints.
2. **Creativity**: AI personas that sound like real hosts, not just text-to-speech.
3. **Observability**: Deep visibility into every decision made by the AI.
4. **Reliability**: A hardened core that never stops broadcasting.

## The Future

Merging all modules (Airwaves, Robo-Rippa) into a single, cohesive "Studio" experience.
